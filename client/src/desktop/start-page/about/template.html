About NonoJs

NonoJs is a free online Nonogram platform. Simply select one of the many Nonograms you see on this front page and get started!

NonoJs is optimized for ease-of-play. Try it on mobile! We believe to have the best nonogram controls available on mobile devices. Even large nonograms, such as 40x40, are fun to play on mobile devices.

We strongly recommend making an account. That way, your savefiles are stored on our server instead of just locally on your computer. In the future, you will be able to play nonograms together with, or against, your friends. Stay tuned!
